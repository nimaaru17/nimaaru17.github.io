Savory Dishes

Paneer Tikka Masala is a North Indian dish made from marinated Paneer, which is India cottage cheese, that is pan-fried or grilled, then simmered in a rich, creamy tomato gravy. The paneer is sliced into smaller cubes and then coated with yogurt and spices, giving them a smoky, tangy flavor. The gravy is also rich with flavors of tomato, garlic, and ginger, making the dish both spicy and comforting at the same time. It is typically eaten with rice or naan (Indian Flatbread). This dish is often made in Indian restaurants and households during festivals and other special occasions.

Gobi Manchurain is a popular Indo-Chinese dish made from deep-fried caulliflower that is cut up and tossed in a spicy, tangy sauce. It combines Indian spices with Chinese flavors like soy sauce, ginger, and garlic. This is usually eaten by itself, with no other side dishes.

Drinks

Falooda is a refreshing Indian desert drink that's made with layers of rose syrup, vermicelli, milk, sweet basil leaves, and is topped with a scoop of vanilla ice cream. It is often drank during the summer because it cools the body down.

Mango Lassi is a famous Indian drink that is enjoyed by all people in India. It is made by blending ripe mangoes with yogurt, sugar, and a hint of cardamom powder. The texture is creamy and the taste is sweet and a little tangy. The drink is cooled down and chilled, offering a delicious way to beat the intense summer heat!
